Need Thai Baht? Cash out Bitcoin using PoS!

Step 1 - Do you have a Bitcoin Wallet?

For those actively using Bitcoin on a daily basis, a mobile BTC wallet is an essential tool. It runs as an app on your smartphone, allowing you to sell Bitcoin directly from the phone.

Step 2 - find the closest bitcoin PoS in your location

If you want to withdraw local currency ( Thai Baht - THB), it is possible at most Exchange offices throughout the country. Absolutely legal, safe and stress-free!

STEP 3 - ASK FOR WITHDRAW

When you locate an Exchange Office, tell the cashier that you want to cash out Bitcoin. Next step is to enter the amount of cash you want to withdraw. Confirm the transaction and wait for a receipt with QR code.

STEP 4 - SCAN QR CODE AND SELL BITCOIN

Using your mobile wallet, scan the QR code to send your bitcoin. If your transactions has received enough confirmations, you will receive your cash.
