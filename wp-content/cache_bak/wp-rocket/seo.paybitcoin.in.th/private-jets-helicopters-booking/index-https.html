Frequently Asked Questions
Come in, have a seat and relax!

Our team of dedicated aviation specialists will fulfill your private jet charter needs and provide you with timely and professional consultations on the most suitable charter aircraft options available.

WHAT DO YOU PROVIDE DURING FLIGHT?

Our aircraft caters for your comfort. Varieties of food, snack and beverages are customized to your likings with pre-order menu. A selection of premium food and wine can also be requested with minimal charges. Special request like dietary restrictions or allergies can be informed before hand. An english-speaking and well-trained flight attendant will be assisting you throughout the flight.

DOES PET ALLOWED ON THE PRIVATE JET FLIGHT?

Small pet is allowed to board the aircraft whereby it is required to keep them in confined space throughout the flight. Certain restriction and conditions from departure and arrival countries may apply. It is advisable to carry your pet certificate of origin and history of vaccination record.

DOES ADVANCE AVIATION JET FLY INTERNATIONALLY?

Yes, our private jet charter routinely flies to thailand’s popular destinations as well as conducts trips to bhutan, china, hong kong, india, indonesia, japan, the maldives, mongolia, myanmar, papua new guinea, the philippines, south korea, taiwan and the uae.

Clientele range from foreign royal family and dignitaries, celebrities, corporate executives and vips to private individuals. Most frequent routes will be within regional south east asia with direct flights to tokyo, all major provinces in china, to qatar and the uae on a westbound or all the way southward to perth or darwin in australia or wherever your business or personal travel takes you. Our G200 Jet range covers the flight time over 6 hours or 3,200 nm.

HOW SOON DO I HAVE TO BOOK MY FLIGHT?

Typically, the earlier the better, as we need to secure slot/permit at each airport.
